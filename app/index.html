Welcome to Re-cycle in Munich

There are many ways to discover a new city. Here at Re-cycle we help you explore a city in an eco-friendly and active way. You will be given the oppertunity to meet other people exploring as well as we will interact with the locals wich will hopefuly give you a new perspective.

The way this works is very simple.You first pick a tour, you can choose between half-day tours, full day tours or a custom tour.

Then you book the tour via the booking page, you will then recive a conformation.

Last step is for you to enjoy the beauties of Munich.
