Lets explore Munich

Munich is a place filled with history, archiecture and beauty, our tours explore all of those aspects no matter what tour you pick. The tours are ofcourse guided by people that know very nock and corner of this city.

English Garden (Half Day)

On this tour we bike through the English Garden, one of the world’s largest urban city park. On this tour we will stop by the some of the famous sights that are hidden within the park. We will stop by; The Japanese Teahouse, Schönfeldwiese and surroundings, Surfing, Monopteros, Steinerne Bank, Chinese Tower, Surroundings, Rumford-Saal, Kleinhesseloher See and lastly Hirschau.And off-course there will the chance to get a beer and pretzel.

Combo Tour (Full Day)

This will be an all-day tour where we will check out both the English Garden and the Japanese Garden as well as the city center tour.

City Centre (Half Day)

On this tour we will bike through the city center of Munich. We will start at Siegestor and then make these stops: Munich Residenz, Marienplatz and the Neues Rathaus, Frauenkirche, Asamkirche, Peterskirche, Nymphenburg Palace, Deutsches Museum and then we will cycle into the Kunstareal District known as Königsplatz where you will get a chance to check out the different art museums.
